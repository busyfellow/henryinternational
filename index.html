About Henry International

A Holding Company Built on Transformation

Henry International is a Dallas–Fort Worth based multinational holding company. We acquire, launch, and systematically grow businesses across service, product, and franchise sectors — each powered by our proprietary digital transformation methodology.

Our competitive advantage is the intersection of Continuous Improvement, BPMN 2.0 process design, and Agentic AI implementation. We don't just invest — we transform the underlying operations of every business we touch.

Every venture in our portfolio is both a revenue-generating asset and a live case study demonstrating what disciplined digital transformation can deliver.
